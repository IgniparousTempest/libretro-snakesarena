In Gimp select Colors->Hue-Chroma...
To get the other player colours, start with the player 1 image and:

player 2: hue -82
player 3: hue +180
player 4: hue -40, lightness: +20, redraw black

In Gimp select Colors->Hue-Saturation...
player 5: hue -120
player 6: hue +90
player 7: hue -88 lightness -100 saturation -54
